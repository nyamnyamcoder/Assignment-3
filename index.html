What we value.

Original & home designed expand_more

At Marlow & Mae, every piece begins at home-designed by founder Rebekah with a focus on originality, quality, and timeless style.

Inspired by her own children, each collection blends vintage charm with practical design.

It’s thoughtful, homegrown fashion made with heart—for little ones and the memories they’ll make.

Ethical & sustainable production expand_more

At Marlow & Mae, every piece is crafted in ethical, sustainable environments across Nepal, China, and India.

We partner only with thoughtful manufacturers who share our values: ensuring fair working conditions, low-impact production, and quality that lasts.

It’s slow fashion made with care, for your little ones and the world they’ll grow up in.

Fast shipping expand_more

Most orders leave our studio within 3–5 days, with express delivery options available.

Need it sooner? Choose express at checkout for 1–3 day delivery within Australia. International orders arrive in as little as 2 weeks.

Every order comes with tracking number, so you’re never left guessing. Speedy, reliable, and stress-free.
